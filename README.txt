The Crowdstrike Filevantage Add-on for Splunk enables you to send requests to Crowdstrike Filevantage Endpoint and indexes the response in JSON.

Crowdstrike currently do not have an add-on for FileVantage. this add-on should help you to collect Filevantage logs.

Install the add-on on IDM or HF and configure the app with your clientId and secret.
